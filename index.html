Ultrasound image segmentation based on SAM and Transformer multi-level feature fusion
Yu Li, Jin Huang
Submitted to IEEE Journal of Biomedical and Health Informatics, 2023.10-2024.5
Code
Medical Image Multimodal LLM
We leveraged the rich semantic segmentation information of SAM and applied its fine-grained attention capability to the feature extraction module of Transformer, achieving SOTA IoU scores in ultrasound image segmentation.

Windowed self-attention guided multi-scale feature stream alignment network for ultrasound image segmentation
Jin Huang, Xiaoxiao Li, Yu Li
Submitted to Biomedical Signal Processing and Control, 2023.9-2024.5
Code
Medical Image
We explored the self-attention guidance mechanism of the Swin-Transformer structure in breast ultrasound images, used a multi-level encoder to deal with multi-dimensional features, and used spatial pyramid pooling and feature stream alignment to fuse shallow and deep information.

Diffusion model with discriminative priors: Self-supervised monocular depth estimation in endoscopy
Yu Li
Under journal submission, 2024.2-2024.6
Code
Medical Image Diffusion
The diffusion model has very good performance in depth estimation and three-dimensional reconstruction scenarios. For endoscopic medical scenarios, we use the diffusion model to enhance the optical appearance reinforcement model. Through the improvement of prior discrimination, the accuracy and confidence of the results are significantly enhanced.

Monte Carlo simulation data is used to improve the model and design the algorithm for the SpO2 measurement process, and finally the reverse judgment process of physiological parameters is realized.
